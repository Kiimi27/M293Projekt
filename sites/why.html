Gaming as a Hobby

Gaming as a Hobby is increasing in Popularity. A lot of people really enjoy submerging themselves in videogame worlds. It lets them do what they want, tackle a hard challenge and beat it, or simulate something really cool like flying a plane.

Why I play videogames

I play videogames mostly to do things I can't do in real life. Some of my best experiences come from games, where the player can explore a world freely. Sometimes this is a colorful, exciting and open world, other times it's a sad, dark and restricting world. These different experiences give many different emotions and let me enjoy them in different times. Sometimes I also play action-fueled shooters or fighting games. These are better for me when I'm calm and concentrated on the game.
